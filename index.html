Pitched Percussion Instruments

Explore the fascinating world of instruments that produce both rhythm and definitive musical tones!

Xylophone

A classic pitched percussion instrument with wooden bars of varying lengths that produce distinct musical notes when struck.

Marimba

Similar to a xylophone but with larger, deeper-toned resonating tubes underneath each bar, creating a richer sound.

Unpitched Percussion Instruments

Discover instruments that create rhythmic sounds without definitive musical pitches!

Bass Drum

A large membranophone that provides deep, thunderous rhythmic foundation in many musical ensembles.

Tambourine

A hand-held instrument with metal jingles that creates crisp, cutting rhythmic sounds when shaken or struck.
